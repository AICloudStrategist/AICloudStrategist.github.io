Template

DPDP vendor register template.

Use this structure to list the tools and partners that process personal data for your business.

Vendor register fields

Capture these fields for every tool, partner, agency, platform, or processor:

  • Vendor name and website.
  • Business owner inside your company.
  • Purpose of processing.
  • Personal data categories handled.
  • User group affected: customers, employees, learners, patients, leads, vendors, or partners.
  • Data location or hosting region, where known.
  • Contract, DPA, or terms link.
  • Retention or deletion expectation.
  • Security evidence available.
  • Risk rating and next review date.

Common vendors to include

Do not limit the register to obvious IT systems. Include CRM, analytics, payment gateways, email marketing, WhatsApp tools, ad platforms, support desks, hosting providers, product analytics, logistics partners, agencies, and spreadsheet-based workflows.
