Consent

DPDP consent flow checklist.

Consent is not only a checkbox. It is a workflow that needs notice quality, capture logic, withdrawal handling, system updates, and evidence.

Review these consent points

  • Where is the notice shown before data collection?
  • Does the notice match actual data use?
  • Which workflows rely on consent?
  • How is consent recorded?
  • How can a person withdraw consent?
  • Which systems need to change after withdrawal?
  • How is marketing consent separated from service communication?
  • How are cookies, analytics, and ad pixels explained?
  • What evidence can the team show later?

Evidence to keep

Keep screenshots of notices, consent UI, form versions, CRM fields, suppression logic, withdrawal logs, and internal owner assignments.
